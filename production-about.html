About the Music Production Course

Course Duration

The Music Production course lasts for 12 weeks, with classes held three times a week.

Cost of the Course

The total cost of the course is $750, which includes access to professional music production software and equipment.

Payment Information

Payments can be made in full or in three installments. For installment payments, $250 is due at the start of the course, $250 after 4 weeks, and the final $250 after 8 weeks.

Career Opportunities

Upon completion of the course, you will have the skills to work as a music producer, sound engineer, or freelancer in the music industry, creating tracks for artists, films, or advertisements.
